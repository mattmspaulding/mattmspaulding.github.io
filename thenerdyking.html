TheNerdyKing

Help Computer Science Students Find Their Passion and Pass Their Classes

Overview

I always thought my passion was for programming. While it is a core passion, I realized (after reflecting on my thought process throughout every project I've worked on) that my true passion is the user, and on their experience. In my experience, most Computer Science students are brought to believe that they will become Software Engineers. While it may be great for some, I don't think it reflects the diversity found within every person. Some people love problem-solving through code, others may love design. TheNerdyKing plans to give an overview of many different areas so students (or anyone!) can find what field matches their skillset and have the necessary resources to help drive them further in that field.

Objective

My plan for the site currently is to launch a beta version. This version will include information and resources for around 10 different topics. I will receive usability feedback through different sites like sprig.com and usertesting.com. This will help me understand anything I may have overlooked, as well as what further information can best help students.

After launch I plan on sending monthly newsletters on resources and deals for students. Ultimately this tool will serve as a collection of resources that have either served myself or others.
