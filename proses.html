Help Writers Visualize Their Stories

Overview

Proses is a project I began working on independently in late August with the hopes of making creative writing a visual experience. It is in the early stages, and I am proud of the work I've done so far. Out of all the projects I've made, I think this project has the most potential, and I am happy to see where it goes.

The Problem

At best, writing tools help users organize their chapters and scenes. They don't help users visualize their stories or enhance productivity and creativty. They expect users to organize their characters and plot threads into plain text files or flashcards.

But what if there was a tool to help writers visually engage with their stories from rough outline to final draft?

The Solution

Proses will help writers keep track of their outlines and progress by visualizing these two core elements:

  • Plot Thread Beats
  • Character Relations

It will also equip writers with different tools for editing and productivity.

Analysis Mode - Highlights adverbs, cliches, and tells.

Senses Mode - Highlights senses.

Flow View - Turns the screen black and disables the use of backspace in hopes the writer can enter the Flow State.

Rhythm View - Turns every sentence into a vertical column on a bar graph. Helps writers see the waves of their writing and how varied their sentences are.

Potential Additional Features:

I have gained access to OpenAI's beta program. Before a public launch, OpenAI must grant approval. Because of this, it can't be guarenteed whether or not these features will make it. Ideally, with the help of OpenAI, Proses will allow users to do the following:

  • Simulate conversations with their characters.
  • Simulate scenes in their story.

There is also a social media aspect I have been thinking about. The first page means everything in a story, and I think the idea of users receiving feedback on this initial impression can be beneficial. Users can be rated on the quality and helpfulness of their feedback. Based on that quality, they can get more points to submit their writing to more people, which encourages thoughtful answers. This is an idea I'm only playing around with at the moment, and would have to see how it fits in with the core identity of Proses once I finish the initial designs.
