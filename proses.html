Help Writers Visualize Their Stories

Overview

Proses is a project I began working on independently in late August with the hopes of making creative writing a visual experience. I want writers to be able to engage with their stories on a deeper level than what other tools allow. The creative writing process hasn't ever evolved into something that is very... well... creative. I want to change that.

The Problem

At best, writing tools help users organize their chapters and scenes. They don't help users visualize their stories or enhance productivity and creativity. They expect users to organize their characters and plot threads into plain text files or flashcards.

Proses helps writers simply write. It minimizes distractions, uses common study method techniques to help writers write in sprints, and gives writers the ability to turn off their screen completely and only regain the ability to see what they produced (as well as the ability to edit their work) once thier goal is complete. For first drafts, this is everything.

The Solution

Proses will help writers keep track of their outlines and progress by visualizing these two core elements:

  • Visual editing
  • Visual outlines (character & plot)
  • Better story navigation
  • Productivity Tools

Proses will equip writers with editing and productivity tools.

Sprint Timers - Set a word count goal and write in short bursts. Reach your goal, take a break, then get back to it!

Flow View - Turns the screen black and disables the use of backspace in hopes the writer can enter the Flow State.

As well as tools to help visualize their stories, whether it be through outlines or through prose.

  • Plot Thread Beats
  • Character Relations

Analysis Mode - Highlights adverbs, cliches, and tells.

Rhythm View - Turns every sentence into a vertical column on a bar graph. Helps writers see the waves of their writing and how varied their sentences are.

I have also gained access to OpenAI's beta program. Before a public launch, OpenAI must grant approval. Because of this, it can't be guaranteed whether or not these features will make it. Ideally, with the help of OpenAI, Proses will allow users to do the following:

Simulate conversations with their characters - Feed dialogue of any character and have a conversation with them. The AI will pick up on their voice and inspire dialogue.

Simulate scenes in their story. - Feed previous scenes to the AI and take turns writing the story. The AI may not create the greatest works, but it will certainly help explore potential exciting new ideas!
