Millie

Help Users Purchase Items They Need

Overview

Automation bots allow users to enter a link to a product that's out-of-stock and automatically purchase it once it is in stock.

Millie was a capstone project I worked on with 2 other students. I worked mainly on the application's front-end and advocated heavily for the user, constantly asking questions on how we could make a bot accessible for users not accustomed to the complexity of similar tools.

My main goal was to help the average user get the items they need.

Objective

The average consumer struggled getting the items they needed during the pandemic. Available bots were complex. They required the user to know what a proxy was and set manual delays on things. The likelihood of the average person knowing what these things even were was low. We wanted to empower this type of user, the average consumer.

The Solution

During an analysis of our competitors, I discovered the strengths and weaknesses of many different automation bots. A commonality in weaknesses was their complexity. My focus throughout the project which I regularly emphasized to the team was to create an interface less complex than other bots, by giving new users simple instructions and default settings while still allowing power users maintain their expected level of control. We wanted to capitalize on targeting average users. With this goal in mind, I created a simple interface that still allowed power users to customize their approach.

Lessons Learned

This project was one of my first experiences working with a team of other developers on a single project. Given the nature of the project, we didn't get to test the software on users, though we did receive positive feedback from our peers (a few even asked for copies of the software). Throughout this process, I found how incredible the development process was when working with a talented team.

Diverse Teams Amplify — It's easy to get absorbed in your own world when you're the only one working on a project. Having other perspectives can help you rethink things you may have overlooked. Having a well-rounded team with different backgrounds can only result in a more well-rounded product.

Communication is Key — Given that this project took place during lockdown, and we were all working remotely on this project, communication was critical. Because of our team's communication, we were able to produce a complete piece of software that fulfilled all our initial requirements.

Agile Works— We used an Agile approach to our development process. Our team met regularly and communicated what we were working on, had finished, and were having trouble on. This really helped us address any potential problems keeping us from delivering on requirements. Additionally, we met with our stakeholder throughout the semester which also helped shape the course of our development.
